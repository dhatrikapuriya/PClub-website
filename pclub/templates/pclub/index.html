Code.Eat.Sleep.Repeat.


Programming Club (PClub) is a community of students where all the students of all semester are highly enthusiastic about coding, web-development, organizing events, Web Designing, Competing in coding competition and many other aspects related to programming. The club was started in the year 2018 by the students of Information and Communication Technology. PClub comes with up the new events, talks, lectures, workshops periodically and makes sure that it serve as gateway for the people to join and explore more in this field. The secret of getting ahead is getting started and the club aims to provide every student with the right start.We also ensure to help and guide the students and thus feel free to contact us through mail for instagram
